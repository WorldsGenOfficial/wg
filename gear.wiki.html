Gear

WorldsGen's main point is gear, with armor and weapons. All of these gears have their own island, and there are always 5 weapons and 5 armors. These gears are always sorted by tiers, meaning that the maximum sword of the first island is Tier 5, since there are always 5. The higher the tier, the better the gear, but sometimes a sword is suddenly worse than before.
